The INITIATIVE CLUB

About Our Club
  • Since: 2017
  • College: Sethu Institute Of Technology
  • Club moto: For the students, By the students

INITIATIVE CLUB was launched in 2017 with the thought -Alone we can do little , together we can do so much . Initiative club moto says- “For the students, By the students” . They paved a way to gain opportunities to learn more about themselves, their goals, strength and also to enhance other’s.

The INITIATIVE CLUB provides wide platform to develop the students Inter/Intra personal skills. As a boon, recognized student club was created for each of the Individual student to develop himself and his/her similar student community and individual leadership. We the juniors express our heartfelt gratitude for proving such an awesome dais –“ THE INITIATIVE CLUB PLATFORM”.

our seniors almost fulfilled the needs of student empowerment in their period.

“The way a team plays as a whole determines its success,you may have the greatest bunch of individual starts in the world" .Our Club looks forward to more awesome Initiatives from the Student empowerment. The Club aprize the talents of students for their best performance in reconstruction of Initiative club Family.

EVENT & DESCRIPTION

  • EVENT-1
    DEBATE
    Non Technical

    The registered participants are separated into two groups and made to sit comfortably facing opposite sides of both.

      • Before starting the event, the juries of the event and chief guests are welcomed ,and the event begins at the given time.

      • Topics are given on the spot.

      • And after receiving the topics, participants can start to debate and compete.

      • A participant can talk minimum of 2minutes, and only after a participant finishes his speech another from the opposite should start to debate. If any interference occurs from any of the participants, they will be banned from the event.

      • Time and venue will be informed later

      • The best two speakers will be awarded, randomly from both sides.
